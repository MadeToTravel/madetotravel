None at all. We had no travel industry background when we started. Full training and support is provided from day one. If you can have a conversation, you have everything you need.
How much time does it take each week?
That is entirely up to you. Many of our partners in Dundee and Tayside start with just a few hours a week around family life. The platform works around you, not the other way around.

No. This is a genuine travel platform with real products that real people use and love. You earn by sharing something that actually saves people money on holidays. There is a clear distinction and we are happy to explain it fully when we chat.
