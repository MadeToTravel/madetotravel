Your Questions Answered

Can I do this alongside full time teaching?
Absolutely. Most of our partners start with just a few hours a week around their existing commitments. The platform is designed to fit around real life, not replace it.
Will this affect my teaching position or contract?
No. This is your own independent business running alongside your career. Many teachers across Scotland run similar income streams without any conflict with their employment.
Is this a pyramid scheme or MLM?
No. This is a genuine travel platform with real products people use and love. You earn by sharing something that genuinely saves people money on holidays. We are happy to explain the full distinction when we chat.
How quickly can I start earning?
That depends on how much time you put in. Some partners see income within their first few weeks. Others build more slowly. We will give you an honest picture of what to expect from day one.

Yes. We run a free weekly Coffee Stop Drop In every Wednesday from 9am to 10.30am at Costa Coffee Monifieth. Just turn up. No booking needed.
